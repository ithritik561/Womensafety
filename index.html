Women Safety Tips

  • Always share your location with a trusted friend or family member.
  • Avoid walking alone in poorly lit areas.
  • Trust your instincts and leave unsafe situations immediately.
  • Keep emergency contacts saved in your phone for quick access.
  • Use safety apps and wear personal safety devices when needed.
